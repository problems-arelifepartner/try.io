Our Capabilities

We provide services to help you engineer, operate, manage or sustain any of the following cybersecurity solutions. If you have a certain product brand in mind, let us know. If not, we are happy to assess your environment and recommend an appropriate tool.

Data Protection

Achieving end-to-end data security requires a blended technological and programmatic approach. Data Loss Prevention (DLP) and Data Protection rely heavily on a formal data security program and governance policy. Phoenix will help you create and refine the processes and procedures you require to avoid compromise, corruption or loss of your data. With sensitive data regularly being accessed on mobile devices or being stored in the cloud, the need for professional data protection services has never been greater. Our data protection services utilize solutions from Forcepoint, Microsoft and Symantec.

Endpoint Security

Endpoint Security is a hot topic. The products, approaches and technologies vary greatly. The lines between Endpoint Protection Platforms (EPP) and Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR) are now blurred; and Big Data and Data Analytics are being used to combat the automation used by the threat actors. These advanced technologies require specific expertise to ensure that your endpoint strategy, tools and processes are comprehensive. Whether you choose an on-premises or cloud-based solution, our services will help you implement industry best practices for Carbon Black, Endgame, and McAfee.

Perimeter Security

Network Security begins at the perimeter. Properly configuring your Next-generation Firewalls (NGFW) is an important part of your initial defense. Now that firewalls include intrusion detection (IDS), intrusion prevention (IPS), antivirus, spam filtering, malware protection, deep-packet inspection and application control, the complexity of Firewall Management has grown considerably. And, the addition of cloud computing has redefined the location of your network’s edge. Phoenix can help you secure your network and cloud applications with services for Cisco, Palo Alto Networks, Forcepoint, Juniper, and Checkpoint.

Phishing Detection & Response

Phishing and spear phishing attacks are some of the most successful techniques for breeching an IT infrastructure. Defending against social engineering requires a combination of technology, threat intelligence, internal policy and end-user training. Our team’s expertise in security strategy, cyber tools, communication processes and policy helps you develop a defense that minimizes the impact on your users and leverages automation to quickly detect and resolve incidents. Our services utilize technologies from Cofense (Phishme), GoPhish, Swimlane and your eMail & Messaging platforms.

Security Automation & Orchestration

We are true believers that automation and orchestration make the role of a security analyst more exciting, operational processes more consistent and your organization more secure. We have had our finger on the pulse of the SAO industry since 2013. Our engineers are expert at the development of integrations, workflows and strategies required to produce the results you expect from a technology this powerful. We are helping organizations take advantage of SAO by delivering lifecycle services for Swimlane.

SIEM

Most large organizations are using Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) technology to collect, analyze and report on their aggregated security data. In our experience with federal agencies, the DoD and large enterprises, we have seen a lot of SIEM data. Our services focus on using this data in an actionable way. SIEMs are getting smarter and more robust. We help our clients take full advantage of these new features, by enhancing their existing processes and fine tuning the way they gather and process their security data. Our expertise includes IBM, LogRhythm, McAfee, Micro Focus, RSA and Splunk.

Threat Intelligence

Whether you choose commercially available threat intelligence services or opensource services, the process for case enrichment is similar. The important part is using the information to better identify an incident’s threat vectors and campaign components; including how the attack may affect other assets on your network. Without automation, the process of gathering this information is very manual. Phoenix has extensive expertise in both the manual and automated processes of enriching your cases and leveraging that data to respond quickly. Our services utilize data feeds from EclecticIQ and Recorded Future.

Threat Hunting

There’s an art and a science to threat hunting. Our team has years of experience building the processes to identify threats before they have the chance to impact your operation. Our services will help you develop your own internal processes and train your team on our best practices. Threat Hunting is an effective way to protect your network, however your analysts typically don’t have time to focus on this task with all their other responsibilities. Our services will help you find that balance, so your analysts are empowered to hunt. We leverage technology from Bricata and Swimlane.

User & Entity Behavior Analytics

Insider Threats and Advanced Persistent Threats are very dangerous and difficult to detect. User & Entity Behavior Analytics (UEBA) applications leverage machine learning, algorithms and statistical analysis to identify rogue users, compromised credentials or machines infected with malware. Securing your intellectual property, preventing fraud and curtailing spear-phishing campaigns are just some of the benefits of a strong UEBA strategy. Phoenix helps protect your sensitive data, enforce compliance policies and reconstruct incident timelines with services for Forcepoint, Splunk and Varonis.

Vulnerability Management

Vulnerability Management is often a task for the IT department, however, exploiting a vulnerability is an easy attack vector for most cybercriminals. Phoenix addresses the need for heightened awareness and specific cybersecurity processes with our assessment, strategy, engineering and operational services. Visibility, end-to-end security, compliance and continuous assessment of your infrastructure are key components. If you prefer to outsource this function, our experts can notify you when assets are vulnerable to attack. Our services utilize tools from Qualys, Tanium and Tenable Network Security.

Electronic Discovery

Available time, tools, and resources influence how organizations design and implement their Electronic Discovery Reference Model (EDRM). Our flexible platform and services help you manage, prioritize, and track eDiscovery requests, as well as automate, orchestrate, and centralize the data collection workflows executed by disparate Electronic Discovery software tools. Our engineers can help select, integrate, and sustain your eDiscovery solution to improve your team’s efficiency, resource allocation, and case management. Phoenix eDiscovery services utilize industry-leading tools including Micro Focus, Microsoft, or Symantec.
